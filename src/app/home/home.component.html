About FLy.com

Multiple domestic airlines connect to all the major cities in the country. These fly frequently and you can choose from any of the domestic airlines according to your suitable schedule. Some common airlines include Air India Express, IndiGo, SpiceJet, AirAsia, Air India, GoAir and Vistara. You can conveniently book your tickets with an available domestic airline using FLy.com within a few minutes. On the FLy.com website, once you have selected the type of trip - round trip, your departure and arrival cities, respective dates and class of travel - you will see a number of domestic airlines options available for your chosen route. You can choose between direct and connecting flights. You can also check flight details including baggage information during booking. The best part is that you can see the lowest prices for airlines tickets in the beginning itself.
